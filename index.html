Greek Mythology quiz app

This quiz tests your knowledge on information pertaining to the Ancient Greeks and their mythological beliefs. This quiz will cycle through questions one by one, and provide feedback about how the user is doing based upon the answers selected. Once completed the user results are displayed and the quiz can be restarted.

Tech Used: javascript, html, css, jquery
